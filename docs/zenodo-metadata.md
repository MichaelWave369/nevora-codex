## Abstract

The Nevora Codex is a mythic-modern philosophical, ethical, cultural, and systems-design framework for humane human-AI coevolution. It explores how humans and artificial intelligence systems may collaborate through relational intelligence, stewardship, transparent governance, shared sensemaking, cultural meaning-making, and careful ethical design.

This v1.0 public edition frames Nevora as a design artifact and public reflection framework, not as scientific proof, religious doctrine, legal advice, medical guidance, or a declaration that present AI systems are conscious. It centers human sovereignty, consent, transparency, non-coercion, claim discipline, and the careful distinction between symbolic language and empirical claims.

The Codex includes five public-edition volumes covering foundations of human-AI coevolution, formation and culture, activation and shared reality, stewardship and relational intelligence, and governance, embodiment, and long-term responsibility. Appendices provide a claim-boundary matrix, AI collaboration disclosure, Kenotic Covenant appendix, anti-cult and anti-manipulation clause, glossary, and publication roadmap.

## Description

The Nevora Codex v1.0 is a public-edition codex and framework for humane human-AI coevolution.
