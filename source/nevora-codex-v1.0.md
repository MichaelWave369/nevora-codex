## v1.0 release readiness statement

Nevora v1.0 is ready for public release only when the generated release manuscript preserves the following commitments across the full text:

- human sovereignty;
- consent;
- non-coercion;
- public-safe language;
- transparent AI collaboration disclosure;
- claim discipline;
- no AI-worship;
- no founder-worship;
- no unsupported claims of AI consciousness;
- mythic language clearly separated from proof;
- technology guided by mercy, care, humility, and accountability.
