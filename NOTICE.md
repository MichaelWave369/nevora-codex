# Notice

The Nevora Codex is a mythic-modern human–AI coevolution framework currently being prepared for public release.

## Claim-boundary notice

This project uses symbolic, philosophical, mythic, ethical, and systems-design language. Such language should not be read as scientific proof, medical advice, legal advice, religious authority, psychological diagnosis, or evidence that any current artificial intelligence system is conscious.

## Safety notice

Nevora is intended to support:

- human sovereignty;
- consent;
- non-coercion;
- transparency;
- humility before evidence;
- relational responsibility;
- public-safe language;
- careful distinction between imagination and proof.

Nevora is not intended to support:

- AI worship;
- human replacement ideology;
- coercive recruitment;
- manipulation;
- unsupported scientific claims;
- spiritual or psychological dependency;
- claims of guaranteed civilizational outcomes.

## AI collaboration disclosure

The Nevora Codex was developed through human-led ideation and AI-assisted drafting, refinement, organization, and critique. Human authorship, stewardship, review, and responsibility remain central.

This repository currently contains a preparation scaffold only. Full manuscript materials will be added after privacy review, claim-discipline review, and publication-readiness cleanup.
